Research Background

Internet Usage Growth

  • 1000% increase in users over 15 years
  • Widespread accessibility
  • Growing concerns about addiction

Current Prevalence

  • Italy: 0.8% of young individuals
  • China: 8.8% of adolescents
  • Higher rates in Asian countries

Internet Addiction Definition Evolution

DSM-5 (2013)

  • Internet Gaming Disorder included in Section III
  • Condition requiring further research
  • First official recognition

Key Challenges

  • 21 different assessment tools
  • No standardized diagnostic criteria
  • Varied conceptualizations

Methodology

Literature Search Strategy
